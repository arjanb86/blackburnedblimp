BLACK BURNED BLIMP

Black Burned Blimp are a rock band from Arnhem, the Netherlands.

The band consists of Eric Broers (vocals and guitar), Arjan Broers (drums), Michaël van der Meer (guitar and keyboards) and Jurjen van Wingerden (bass).

Sounding like a speedfreakin' monster having a visit in disneyworld, Black Burned Blimp combines experimental rock (with a pinch of punk) with the heavy sound of stoner and top it of with some psychedelics here and there.
